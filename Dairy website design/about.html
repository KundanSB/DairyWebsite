About Us

KUNDAN DAIRY Ltd. deals in milk and milk products under our “Dairy Power” brand with our motto ‘Real Power with Real Taste’ since year 2008. Dairy Power Ltd. is established as a partnership firm, registered under the Indian Partnership Act,1932.

We are driven by our vision of “providing remunerative returns to the milk producer and also serving and satisfying the interest of consumers by supplying quality products that are wholesome and value for money while fully ensuring consistent adherence to quality and standard parameters laid down by the concerned competent authorities.”
